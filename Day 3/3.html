ॐ देवी चंद्रघण्टायै नमः    

Form of Chandraghanta
On this third day of Navratri, the Goddess Chandraghanta Devi is worshipped.
She is depicted with a third eye on her forehead and a crescent moon on her head. She usually has ten hands that hold various weapons and symbols.
Name:
The bell of the moon.
Rules:
Manipura Chakra and Lord Sun.
Color:
Associated with Yellow.
Marigold:
Used in the decoration and offerings.
Fasting:
Devotees observe a fast on this day.

  -- Jai maa Devi --
