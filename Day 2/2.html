THE 9 DEVI'S POWER!

1

SHAILAPUTRI

(Daughter of the Mountain)

• Power: Fertility and Growth

EXAMPLE :

- Just as the earth is fertile and gives life, Shailaputri represents the source of growth and abundance.

2

Brahmacharini

(The Devotee of Lord Brahma)

• Power: Purity and Tapas (Austerity)

EXAMPLE :

Like a devoted ascetic, she symbolizes discipline and penance.

3

Chandraghanta

(The One with the Half-Moon Shaped Bell)

Power: Courage and Protection

EXAMPLE :

Her fierce form is like a protective shield against evil.

4

KUSHMANDA

(The Creator of the Universe)

• Power: Creative Energy

EXAMPLE :

She is the cosmic source of energy, much like the sun's creative power on Earth.

5

Skandamata

(The Mother of Skanda/Kartikeya)

• Power: Maternal Love

EXAMPLE :

Her love for her son, Skanda, represents the nurturing and protective nature of motherhood.

6

KATYAYANI

(The Warrior Goddess)

• Power: Bravery and Valor

EXAMPLE :

She embodies the courage to fight against adversity, much like a fearless warrior.

7

KAALRATRI

(The Dark Goddess)

• Power: Protection from Evil

EXAMPLE :

She is the fierce protector who destroys negativity, much like a powerful storm clears the air.

8

MAHAGAURI

(The Extremely Fair Goddess)

• Power: Purity and Serenity

EXAMPLE :

Her serene form represents purity of heart and mind.

9

SIDDHIDATRI

(The Bestower of Siddhis - Supernatural Powers)

• Power: Blessing and Knowledge

EXAMPLE :

She grants divine knowledge and supernatural abilities to her devotees.
